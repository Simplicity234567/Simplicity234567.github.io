Welcome to the 1st Paratrooper Division

Are you an adventurer seeking excitement and challenge? Then join the 1st Paratrooper Division of the GTAF! As a GTAF paratrooper, you will have the opportunity to play a vital and exciting role in the Air Force.
As a member of the 1st Division, you will be trained to conduct raids behind enemy lines, wreaking havoc and disrupting enemy forces. This will require courage, determination, and aerial combat skills.
In addition to being an exceptional athlete, you must be able to work as a team and have a leadership mindset. If you have what it takes to become a GTAF paratrooper, then apply today! We will give you the chance to serve your country and live the adventurous life you've always dreamed of.
Requirements
  • Basic English
  • Maturity
  • Being active
  • Decent combat skills
